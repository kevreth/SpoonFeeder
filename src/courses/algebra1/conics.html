Circle draw(Circle((0,0),1)); \( x^2 + y^2 = r^2 \) \( \frac{x^2}{r^2} + \frac{y^2}{r^2} = 1 \) \( (x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 = r^2 \)
Ellipse (Horizontal) draw(shift((0,0))*scale(2,1)*Circle((0,0),1)); \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{(x - h)^2}{a^2} + \frac{(y - k)^2}{b^2} = 1 \)
Ellipse (Vertical) draw(shift((0,0))*scale(1,2)*Circle((0,0),1)); \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{(x - h)^2}{a^2} + \frac{(y - k)^2}{b^2} = 1 \)
Hyperbola (Horizontal) draw(shift((0,0))*scale(1,1)*hyperbola((0,0),2,1)); \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} - \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} - \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{(x - h)^2}{a^2} - \frac{(y - k)^2}{b^2} = 1 \)
Hyperbola (Vertical) draw(shift((0,0))*scale(1,1)*hyperbola((0,0),1,2,90)); \( \frac{y^2}{b^2} - \frac{x^2}{a^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{y^2}{b^2} - \frac{x^2}{a^2} = 1 \) \( \frac{(y - k)^2}{b^2} - \frac{(x - h)^2}{a^2} = 1 \)
Parabola (Vertical) draw(shift((0,0))*graph(y=x^2,-2,2)); \( y = ax^2 + bx + c \) Not applicable for parabolas \( y = a(x - h)^2 + k \)
Parabola (Horizontal) draw(shift((0,0))*parametricplot((t^2,t),-2,2)); \( x = ay^2 + by + c \) Not applicable for parabolas \( x = a(y - k)^2 + h \)
