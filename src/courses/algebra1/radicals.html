Law Name Formula (in MathJax)
Product of Radicals \( \sqrt[n]{a} \times \sqrt[n]{b} = \sqrt[n]{a \times b} \)
Quotient of Radicals \( \frac{\sqrt[n]{a}}{\sqrt[n]{b}} = \sqrt[n]{\frac{a}{b}} \)
Power of a Radical \( (\sqrt[n]{a})^n = a \)
Radical of a Power \( \sqrt[n]{a^m} = a^{\frac{m}{n}} \)
Nested Radical \( \sqrt[m]{\sqrt[n]{a}} = \sqrt[mn]{a} \)
